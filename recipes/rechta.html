ODIN RECIPE

rechta

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • rechta sheets
  • 200g poulet
  • 50g grated parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh basil (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and cook chopped onions and garlic until soft.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Add canned tomatoes and tomato paste. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. In a baking dish, layer meat sauce, lasagna sheets, and cheese. Repeat layers.
  6. Top with mozzarella and grated parmesan.
  7. Bake in the oven for 30-40 minutes or until golden and bubbling.
  8. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
